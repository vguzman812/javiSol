Who I Am
Javi Soler

Hello, and welcome! I'm Javi, a Miami native with a decade-long journey of capturing the ethereal beauty of coral reefs through my lens. But photography isn't just an art; it's a statement. A statement of how crucial coral conservation is in today's rapidly changing world. Every photograph I take is an opportunity to inspire, educate, and mobilize people to protect these underwater havens.
